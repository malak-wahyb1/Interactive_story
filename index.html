The last camping

Four friends descided to go on a camping trip. while wondering around, they discovered that the place is full with zombies and cannibals, so they are trying to escape alive . Help them escape from the forest or they will die.

Script

One day, four friends planning to go on a holiday, they descided to go camping. While wondering around in the woods, they foundout that the forest is full with zombies and cannibals, and now they are trying to escape . The four friends have three ways to escape, either run throught the forest , or go back to camp and get the car keys and run or go inside an abandoned house and take cover in it. In the first case, while crossing the forest one of the friends got infected, and they have two choices, either they kill their friend and escape or he will infect them also and the game is over. The second case, they go back to the tent to get the keys and run, but, the tent is sorrounded with these creatures and one of them will have to sacrifice himself distract them to get the keys and run or they will die. Last but not least, they go inside the house, to find out that its their headquarters and they found inside a good amout of ammunition, they fight and win or they loose the ammunition and die.
